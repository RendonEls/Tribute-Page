Leonardo DaVinci

Timeline of the original renaissance man, Leonardo DaVinci.

  • 1452: ·Born On April 15, In Vinci. Leonardo was born to Ser Piero, a notary, and Caterina, a peasant girl; his parents were never married.

  • 1467: ·Becomes An Apprentice To Andrea Del Verrocchio, In Florence. This date is an estimate, the apprenticeship could have begun a year or two later. But even 1467 would have been a bit late according to the customs of the time: Leonardo would have been fifteen, and most apprentices began at a younger age.

  • 1472: ·Becomes A Member Of The Florence Painters' Guild.

  • 1473: ·Draws A Landscape On The Feast Of Santa Maria Delle Neve. This is his earliest known drawing, though he probably also had a hand in Verrochio's Baptism of Christ, which probably reached completion in or around 1473.

  • 1477: ·Had At Least Some Part In The Painting The Annunciation To The Virgin, Completed Around This Time.

  • 1481: ·Receives A Commission To Paint The Adoration Of The Magi. The painting was never completed, yet was–and is–considered a masterpiece. This same year, most of the other noteworthy painters in Florence were sent to Rome to work on projects for the Pope.

  • 1482: ·Moves From Florence To Milan, Entering The Patronage Of Ludovico Sforza.

  • 1483: ·Receives A Commission To Paint The Virgin Of The Rocks.

  • 1489: ·Studies Anatomy.

  • 1490: ·Begins Work In Earnest On The Bronze Horse For His Patron Ludovico Sforza. Leonardo Also Begins A Book On Landscape And Hydraulic Works; It Is Never Finished.

  • 1495: ·Begins His Last Supper In The Convent Of Santa Maria Delle Grazie.

  • 1496: ·Illustrates Mathematician Fra Luca Pacioli's De Divina Proportione.

  • 1498: ·Decorates The Walls And Ceiling Of The Sala Delle Asse. He Also Made His First Attempts At Planning A Flying Machine Around

  • 1500: ·And Pacioli Go To Mantua, Then Leonardo Continues On To Florence.

  • 1502: ·Becomes Cesare Borgia's Military Engineer.

  • 1503: ·Back In Florence, Leonardo Begins The Battle Of Anghiari.

  • 1505: ·Makes A Second Attempt To Build A Flying Machine And Begins Sketches For The Mona Lisa, Which He Completes Sometime Later.

  • 1506: ·In May, Leonardo Is Summoned To Milan By Charles D'Amboise, The French Governor.

  • 1507: ·Is Appointed Louis XII's Painter And Engineer

  • 1513: ·Moves To Rome.

  • 1515: ·Around This Time, Leonardo Paints The Painting John The Baptist.

  • 1516: ·Permanently Leaves Italy For France, Where He Will Serve Francis I In His Court In Amboise.

  • 1519: ·In May, Leonardo Dies At Cloux.
